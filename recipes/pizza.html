Steps to make the pizza

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water in a bowl
  3. add flour, oil and salt to the yeasy mixture
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface
  6. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan
  7. spread crust with sauce
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
